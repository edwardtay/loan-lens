# LoanLens Demo Guide

## üé¨ Recording Your Demo Video

### Video Requirements
- **Length**: 2-3 minutes maximum
- **Format**: MP4, MOV, or upload to YouTube/Vimeo
- **Quality**: 1080p recommended
- **Audio**: Clear narration explaining features

### Recommended Demo Flow

#### 1. Opening (15 seconds)
**Script**: 
> "Loan agreements are complex, multi-hundred-page documents that exist as unstructured text. LoanLens transforms them into actionable intelligence in seconds."

**Show**: 
- Landing page with hero section
- Highlight the problem statement

#### 2. Document Upload & Analysis (45 seconds)
**Script**: 
> "Let me show you how it works. I'll load a demo facility agreement - a $500 million revolving credit facility."

**Actions**:
1. Click "Load Demo Document" button
2. Wait for analysis (2-3 seconds)
3. Dashboard appears automatically

**Highlight**:
- Summary cards showing:
  - $500M facility amount
  - Term SOFR + 250bps rate
  - 10 covenants detected
  - 4 risk flags
- Parties section (borrowers, lenders, agents)
- Financial terms breakdown
- Key dates (maturity, effective date)

**Script**: 
> "In seconds, LoanLens has extracted all parties, financial terms, covenants, and key dates. Notice it detected 3 financial covenants including leverage ratio and interest coverage requirements."

#### 3. ESG & Risk Analysis (30 seconds)
**Actions**:
1. Scroll to ESG section
2. Show sustainability-linked features
3. Scroll to Risk Analysis section

**Script**: 
> "LoanLens automatically identifies ESG provisions. This loan is sustainability-linked with carbon reduction KPIs and a margin ratchet. The risk analysis flagged 4 high-priority items: cross-default provisions, MAC clause, change of control, and acceleration clauses."

#### 4. Covenant Compliance Calculator (30 seconds)
**Actions**:
1. Navigate to compliance calculator (if implemented in UI)
2. Input sample metrics:
   - Leverage Ratio: 3.2
   - Interest Coverage: 4.5
   - Net Worth: 120000000

**Script**: 
> "Here's where it gets powerful. Input your actual financial metrics and LoanLens calculates real-time covenant compliance. With a leverage ratio of 3.2, we're compliant but only 0.3 away from the 3.5 threshold - that's a warning status."

#### 5. Multi-Loan Comparison (20 seconds)
**Actions**:
1. Navigate to Compare view
2. Show loan selection
3. Display comparison table

**Script**: 
> "Compare multiple loans side-by-side. LoanLens automatically detects inconsistencies like currency mismatches, different reference rates, or ESG provision variations across your portfolio."

#### 6. Closing (10 seconds)
**Script**: 
> "LoanLens addresses four LMA Edge Hackathon categories: Digital Loans, Loan Documents, Keeping Loans on Track, and Greener Lending. Built with security-first architecture and zero vulnerabilities. Check it out on GitHub."

**Show**: 
- GitHub URL: https://github.com/edwardtay/loan-lens
- LMA Edge Hackathon logo/badge

### Tips
- Use a good microphone or headset
- Record in a quiet environment
- Practice your script 2-3 times before recording
- Keep cursor movements smooth and deliberate
- Pause briefly between sections for easier editing
- Add background music (optional, keep it subtle)
